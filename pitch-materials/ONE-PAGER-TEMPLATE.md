## DESIGN TIPS

### Keep It:
- **One Page**: No scrolling required
- **Scannable**: Use bullets, bold text, icons
- **Professional**: Clean fonts, consistent spacing
- **Branded**: Add logo, use brand colors

### Font Sizes:
- Company Name: 24-32pt
- Section Headers: 16-18pt
- Body Text: 10-12pt
- Footer: 8-10pt

### Layout:
- Margins: 0.5 inches all around
- Line spacing: 1.0 or 1.15
- Column layout: Single column for easy reading
- White space: Don't cram too much
