## 🎨 CREATE YOUR POWERPOINT DECK

### Step 1: Choose Your Tool
- **PowerPoint** (Microsoft Office)
- **Google Slides** (Free, collaborative)
- **Keynote** (Mac only, beautiful)
- **Canva** (Online, templates available)

### Step 2: Set Up Presentation
1. Open PowerPoint
2. Design → Slide Size → Widescreen (16:9)
3. Choose a clean, professional template OR start blank

### Step 3: Add Content
1. Open `POWERPOINT-TEMPLATE.md`
2. Copy content slide by slide
3. Apply consistent formatting
4. Insert screenshots where indicated
5. Add your branding (logo, colors)

### Step 4: Customize
- **Slide 14 (Team)**: Add your actual team bios
- **Slide 15 (Contact)**: Update with real contact info
- **Slide 5, 6, 7**: Insert your screenshots
- **Colors**: Use Alogix purple (#667eea, #764ba2)

### Step 5: Export
- **For Email**: File → Export → PDF
- **For Presenting**: Save as .pptx
- **For Web**: Export as images

**Time Required**: 2-3 hours

## 🎬 RECORD YOUR VIDEO DEMO

### Step 1: Prepare
1. Read `VIDEO-DEMO-SCRIPT.md` thoroughly
2. Set up recording software (Loom recommended)
3. Prepare demo data in your TMS
4. Test audio and video quality

### Step 2: Practice
- Read through script 2-3 times
- Navigate your TMS while reading
- Time yourself (target: 5-7 minutes)
- Adjust pacing as needed

### Step 3: Record
1. Close unnecessary apps/tabs
2. Turn off notifications
3. Hit record
4. Follow the script (don't memorize, be natural)
5. Pause and re-record sections if needed

### Step 4: Edit & Polish
- Trim dead air at start/end
- Add intro/outro slides (5 seconds each)
- Blur sensitive data if needed
- Add captions (optional but recommended)

### Step 5: Publish
- Upload to YouTube (unlisted) or Vimeo (private)
- Get shareable link
- Add to pitch deck and website

**Time Required**: 2-3 hours (including editing)

## 💡 PITCHING TIPS

### Before the Meeting:
1. **Send deck 24 hours ahead**: "Looking forward to our call. Here's our deck for context."
2. **Include video**: "5-min demo video if you'd like to see the product first."
3. **Research the investor**: Tailor 1-2 slides to their interests

### During the Meeting:
1. **Start with the problem** (Slide 2) - make them feel the pain
2. **Show, don't tell** - Use War Room screenshot or live demo
3. **Highlight multi-MC** (Slide 4) - This is your differentiation
4. **Know your numbers cold** - CAC, LTV, churn, ARR projections
5. **Tell a story** - "Last week, a carrier with 50 trucks told us..."

### After the Meeting:
1. **Send thank you email** within 24 hours
2. **Include deck PDF** as reminder
3. **Share demo video link** again
4. **Offer follow-up demo** for their team

## 🔧 TROUBLESHOOTING

### HTML Deck Not Displaying Correctly:
- **Solution**: Open in Chrome or Firefox (not Internet Explorer)
- Ensure JavaScript is enabled
- Try opening in Incognito mode

### Screenshots Look Blurry in PowerPoint:
- **Solution**: Insert as "Original Size" not "Fit to Slide"
- Use PNG format, not JPG
- Capture at 1920x1080 or higher

### Video File Too Large:
- **Solution**: Use Handbrake to compress
- Target bitrate: 5-10 Mbps
- Resolution: 1080p (1920x1080)

### Can't Export PowerPoint to PDF:
- **Solution**: Use Google Slides → File → Download → PDF
- OR use online converter: smallpdf.com
