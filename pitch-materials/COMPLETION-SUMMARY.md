## 💡 PRO TIPS

### Before You Start Outreach:

1. **Practice Your Pitch**
   - Rehearse 10 times minimum
   - Time yourself (aim for 5-7 minutes)
   - Record yourself on video
   - Get feedback from 3-5 people

2. **Know Your Numbers Cold**
   - LTV, CAC, churn, burn rate
   - Market size (TAM/SAM/SOM)
   - Customer acquisition cost
   - Revenue per customer

3. **Prepare for Q&A**
   - "Why are you better than [competitor]?"
   - "What's your go-to-market strategy?"
   - "How do you defend against [competitor] copying you?"
   - "What's your unit economics story?"
   - "Why now? Why you?"

4. **Research Your Investors**
   - What stage do they invest in? (Seed, Series A)
   - Do they invest in logistics/SaaS?
   - What's their check size? ($250K-$1M?)
   - Who else have they invested in?
   - What's their typical deal structure?
